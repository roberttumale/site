After Ma’am Wenda gave the instructions, we collected the materials and returned to our group. When the timer started, we worked together and tried to communicate clearly, but the time pressure made us rush. Reflection

The marshmallow tower challenge was a fun but difficult activity that taught us important lessons about teamwork and planning. We felt a lot of pressure because the time was limited, so we ended up cramming and rushing our work. Because of this, our base was not strong enough and the spaghetti pieces were not connected well, causing the tower to collapse when we placed the marshmallow on top. This showed us that a strong foundation is very important and that we should test our structure early. We also learned that good communication and cooperation are necessary because working together helps us solve problems faster. Although we failed, the activity helped us understand how to manage pressure, plan better, and improve our problem-solving skills for future challenges.
