OVERALL REFLECTION

Building the prototype right after the persona mapping felt like the perfect next step. Because we already understood the students' and librarians' exact frustrations, we didn’t waste any time guessing what features to create. We knew exactly what shortcuts to build to solve their stress. For the prototype, we focused heavily on making things instant and visual. We designed a simple dashboard showing a live, color-coded capacity map of the library floors so students could spot open seats before even walking through the door. Turning our persona insights into a clickable reality proved that good design isn't about being fancy—it’s about making a chaotic day a little easier for the people using it.
